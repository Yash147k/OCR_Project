How To Use It . . .

Welcome to the software guide for our text extraction web app! This guide aims to help you make the most of our web app's powerful features for extracting text from both images and PDF files. Whether you need to extract text from scanned documents, photographs, or PDFs, our web app provides a convenient and efficient solution. Say goodbye to manual typing and tedious data entry tasks! By leveraging advanced optical character recognition (OCR) technology, our web app accurately detects and extracts text from images and PDFs, saving you time and effort.

To ensure the best results, it is recommended to use clear and high-quality images for the extraction process. Clear images with good resolution and minimal noise will enhance the accuracy of the text extraction. Avoid images that are too blurry, low-resolution, or contain excessive background noise, as these factors may impact the extraction results. We have different modes of text extraction for different types of images. You can choose one and use it for the extraction.

Default mode - general mode for simple text images, gives good results when text is clear.
Vertical mode - mode for vertical text extraction, gives best results for vertical text among others.
Scanned mode - best for scanned or doc photograph like images.
Distorted mode - perfect when your image have text not in line or for simple captcha that has clear background.
Hindi mode - this is our additional feature to extract hindi language text from images that contains hindi.
All o/p - use this mode when you want all mode outputs, the extracted text will be in form of output-0 to output-4.

In addition to the text extraction features, our web app also integrates a powerful snipping tool to facilitate the extraction process. With the snipping tool, you can precisely select a specific portion of an image to extract text from, providing even more control and flexibility. When you click the snip button, the snipping tool overlays a selection box on the image, allowing you to define the exact area from which you want to extract text. Simply drag and resize the selection box to encompass the desired portion of the image.

To ensure optimal results, it is recommended to make the snipping box slightly larger than the desired text area. This helps to capture any surrounding context or additional characters that may improve the accuracy of the extracted text. By including some extra space around the text, you can enhance the performance of the text extraction process. Utilize the snipping tool alongside the text extraction features to precisely extract text from specific regions of an image, enabling you to focus on the areas of interest and obtain the most accurate results for your text extraction needs.

Our web app also offers seamless text extraction from PDF files, providing you with the ability to extract text from individual pages or the entire document. Whether you need to extract text from a single page or navigate through multiple pages for extraction, our web app offers a convenient solution. To extract text from PDF files, simply upload the PDF document to the web app. You can then choose to extract text from a specific page or opt for extracting text from the entire document. Our web app supports efficient navigation through PDF pages, allowing you to move forward or backward to access the desired pages for extraction.

Additionally, the snipping tool functionality is also available for PDF files, enabling you to precisely select a portion of the PDF page and extract text from that specific area. By leveraging the snipping tool within the PDF viewer, you can capture and extract text from precise regions of interest within the PDF document. With our comprehensive PDF text extraction capabilities, you can extract text from PDF files with ease, whether it's a single page or the entire document. Combine this functionality with the flexibility of the snipping tool, and you have a powerful toolset to extract text from PDFs with precision and accuracy.

By leveraging advanced technologies such as optical character recognition (OCR) and the integration of a powerful snipping tool, our web app empowers you to effortlessly extract text from various sources, enhancing productivity and saving you valuable time. We encourage you to take advantage of the different modes of text extraction available in our web app, such as default, vertical, scanned, distorted, Hindi, or the all-inclusive mode. Experiment with the snipping tool to capture precise portions of images or PDF pages for optimal text extraction results.
